## ğŸ“ Key Design Decisions

1. **HTTP-only Cookies**: Prevents XSS attacks on tokens
2. **Separate Access/Refresh Tokens**: Better security, can revoke refresh tokens
3. **Single Role per User**: Simplified for MVP, can evolve later
4. **Single Entity per User**: One primary assignment (zone/state/org/kvk)
5. **Soft Delete**: Users marked as deleted, not removed from DB
6. **bcrypt for Passwords**: Industry standard, 12 rounds
7. **JWT for Tokens**: Stateless, scalable, standard approach
